Services overview

UNISON BATTERIES(I) PVT LTD, is a pioneer in the heavy electrical segments, which produces batteries for light automobiles, cars, heavy vehicles and inverters. At Unison Batteries, we aim to provide powerful, durable, long lasting batteries which are also light on the pocket and require low maintenance. A synonym of endurance and reliability, Unison Batteries is on a mission to power India with the best batteries at affordable costs. But the list doesn't end here. Being among the top battery manufacturers also demands a sense of responsibility towards nature and environment. At Unison, we adopt eco-friendly manufacturing processes for batteries to ensure minimum impact on the environment and preserve the surrounding ecosystem. Apart from this, we also have a battery recycling facility to ensure safe and clean disposal of used batteries, while recycling raw materials and conserving resources.
